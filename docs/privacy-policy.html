Briefly is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

  • Telegram account identifier

Connected Source Information

  • Telegram channels and groups selected by the user

3. How We Use Information

  • Generate summaries and insights

4. AI Processing

Content processed through Briefly may be analyzed by artificial intelligence systems. Depending on configuration, AI processing may be performed using self-hosted AI models or trusted third-party AI providers.

5. Google User Data

  • We access Gmail data only with your authorization.
  • Gmail data is used solely to generate summaries, insights, and digest functionality requested by you.
  • We do not sell Gmail data.
  • We do not use Gmail data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as necessary to provide the Service.
  • Google user data is not used to train generalized artificial intelligence models.
  • Access permissions may be revoked by the user at any time.

6. LinkedIn Data

  • We access only data authorized through LinkedIn permissions.
  • LinkedIn content is used exclusively to provide monitoring, analysis, and summarization features requested by the user.
  • We do not sell LinkedIn data.

7. Telegram Data

  • We may access channels, groups, and content selected by the user.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with infrastructure providers, cloud hosting providers, AI processing providers, or legal authorities when required by law.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information, including encryption of sensitive credentials, secure storage of authentication tokens, access controls, monitoring, and logging.

10. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion at any time.

11. Your Rights

Depending on infrastructure providers and integrations used, user data may be processed outside the user's country of residence.
